SHINSEI VILLAGE

VISION

Shinsei Village is a collection featuring 5,000 demigod monkeys, unraveling a mysterious past in their village. The project is not just a PFP collection, but a fascinating on-chain storyline. Through a focus on community engagement, the project seeks to build the new era of Web3 entertainment.

Each stage of development is aimed to deliver the adventure experience to the community, as well as providing rewards and benefits to all holders: solve weekly puzzles to open mystic boxes and collect $CLAW. Uncovering the plot is entirely up to the care and decisions of the community.
